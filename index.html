You’re looking for a copywriter to add to your ‘inner circle’.

Why not add an additional tech wizz to that circle that will complement Tega? And while you're at it... Why not add a pretty competent illustrator to the circle, too?

What would THAT do to your online social status?

You don’t have space for three more people, you say?

Not a problem.

You see, I am all three packed into one. A copywriter, a tech wizz, and an illustrator.

(I have 16 years of software development and web dev on my back, and studying classical drawing at LARA.)

Bam! A winner.
